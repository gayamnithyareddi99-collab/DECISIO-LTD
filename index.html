About Decisio Ltd

Decisio Ltd is a UK-based decision intelligence company focused on enabling organisations to move beyond reactive reporting toward predictive and optimised decision-making.

Across UK SMEs and operational organisations, significant volumes of data are generated daily, yet critical decisions continue to rely on historical dashboards and manual judgement. Decisio was established to bridge this gap by embedding advanced analytics directly into operational workflows.

The Decisio platform integrates automated data ingestion, predictive modelling, explainable analytics, and optimisation logic within a single cloud-native system designed for practical, real-world use.

The Problem

Despite increasing data availability, most organisations remain fundamentally reactive in how decisions are made.

Operational decisions are still driven by historical reports, disconnected dashboards, and manual judgement rather than forward-looking intelligence.

Critical decisions are made after outcomes have already occurred, limiting the ability to prevent cost overruns, inefficiencies, or service degradation.

Existing analytics tools focus on visualisation rather than decision support, leaving leaders without clear guidance on what actions to take.

Advanced analytics capabilities remain inaccessible to most organisations due to complexity, cost, and reliance on specialised technical expertise.

Decisio Operating Model

A structured, governance-aware decision intelligence lifecycle designed to support reliable, auditable, and scalable decision-making for UK organisations.

01

Data Intake & Governance

Secure ingestion of operational data with built-in validation, lineage tracking, and governance controls to ensure compliance, auditability, and decision integrity.

02

Analytical Intelligence

Predictive models and anomaly detection engines analyse historical and real-time data to identify emerging trends, risks, and performance drivers.

03

Scenario Modelling

Alternative operational scenarios are simulated to evaluate potential outcomes across cost, capacity, service, and regulatory constraints.

04

Decision Optimisation

Prescriptive optimisation techniques prioritise recommended actions, enabling informed decision-making aligned with organisational objectives.

05

Execution Enablement

Decisions are delivered through intuitive dashboards and alerts, embedded into operational workflows for timely execution by business teams.

06

Feedback & Continuous Learning

Outcomes are monitored and fed back into the system to continuously improve model accuracy, relevance, and organisational learning.
